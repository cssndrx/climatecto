Are you starting a climate startup? We want to empower all climate startups to succeed by giving them access to the highest quality technical talent from Silicon Valley.

When we left our jobs as Google engineers in July 2020, half a dozen climate startups reached out and asked us to be their CTO. We realized that the lack of network overlap between those with deep domain expertise in climate and those with deep experience in software engineering, is preventing some climate startups from taking off.

We want to bridge this gap by finding you a climate CTO. All candidates are either from our networks, or have been vetted and screened by us.

FAQ

For individuals
What do I get by signing up?

Fill in our talent intake form, describing your skills and desires, and we will email you if we see a good fit. You can also opt-in to cofounder mixers and events that we host.

Is this free?

Yes. We want to help you find a meaningful job in climate and believe that this is one of the best ways to scale our impact.

What can I do while I wait to be matched?

Get started taking action today by finding people and resources with others in the Work on Climate community.

For organizations
What do I get by signing up?

Fill in our organization intake form describing your project and team. If we see a skills match, we will send you candidate profiles.

Is this free?

Yes. You are doing critical work for our world, and we want to empower you with great talent. We hope you do great things and pay it forward.
