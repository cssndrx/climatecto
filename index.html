FAQ

For individuals
What do I get by signing up?

Fill in our talent intake form, describing your skills and desires, and we will email you if we see a good fit. You can also opt-in to cofounder mixers and events that we host.

Is this free?

We are free for potential CTOs. We want to help you find a meaningful job in climate and believe that this is one of the best ways to scale our impact. Companies pay us a voluntary placement fee if you end up joining them fulltime.

What can I do while I wait to be matched?

Get started taking action today by finding people and resources with our sister organization Work on Climate.

For organizations
What do I get by signing up?

Fill in our organization intake form describing your project and team. If we see a skills match, we will send you candidate profiles. We help you source talent and interview candidates, because we want to empower climate organizations with the best candidates.

Is this free?

We offer this service to organizations under a “pay as you can model” in order to balance our desire to assist as many climate companies as possible with the desire to run a sustainable operation.
